Digital Material Identifer (DMI)c back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/dmi/Digital_material_identifier

A dmi:DigitalMaterialIdentifier is a resource identifier (URI or IRI) that represents the specification of a material (in the sense of a material class) described in a bibliographic resource or specification document. The specification document can be, e.g., a standard, a datasheet, or any other citable bibliographic resource. Typically, a specification document requires some qualities that a material (in the sense of a portion of matter) has to comply with. Typical requirements include chemical compositions or physical properties of the material.
has super-classes
is in domain of
prov:wasQuotedFrom op, skos:altLabel, skos:prefLabel
has members
2618A (example) ni, EN AW-2618A (example) ni, Fuchs AN40 (example) ni
is disjoint with
dcterms:Agent c, dcterms:Bibliographic Resource c
